What We Do

Six services. One crew you can reach.

We run our own trucks and equipment on every job — excavation, hauling, and site prep for homeowners and contractors across Lewis, Thurston, and Cowlitz County.

Excavation & Dirt Work

Foundation dig-outs, septic system excavation, culvert installation, and general trenching and grading for residential and commercial builds.

Typical jobs: new home foundations, septic systems, driveway approaches, and water, sewer & electrical trenching.

Site Development

Full site prep — land grading and leveling, building pad preparation, and drainage planned and handled right from day one.

Includes erosion control and utility trenching coordination. We work directly with builders, contractors, and property owners.

Road Building

Private roads and access routes cut, graded, rocked, and drained so you can reach every corner of your property year-round.

From new access roads on rural acreage to road and driveway base prep for developments.

Trucking & Hauling

Material import & export — gravel, sand, and fill delivery, dirt import and export, rock and aggregate hauling, and construction debris removal.

Fleet GVW capacity up to 55,500 lbs. Same-day loads scheduled when the truck's free.

Land Clearing

Tree and brush removal, stump grinding, and lot or acreage clearing to get raw ground ready to build on.

Improves usability and value, cuts fire hazard, and preps land for construction or agriculture — debris haul-off included.

Gravel Driveways

New driveway installs and re-grading with proper drainage so they hold up through winter.

We grade for water runoff first — most driveway failures start with drainage, not gravel depth.
