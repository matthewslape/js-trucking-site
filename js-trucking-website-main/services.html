What We Do

Six services. One crew you can reach.

We run our own trucks and equipment on every job — excavation, hauling, and site prep for homeowners and contractors across Southwest Washington.

Excavation

Foundations, basements, utility trenching, and site grading for residential and commercial builds.

Typical jobs: new home foundations, basement digs, septic and drainage trenching, driveway culverts.

Trucking & Hauling

Dump truck service for gravel, topsoil, fill dirt, debris removal, and material delivery.

Fleet GVW capacity up to 55,500 lbs. Same-day loads scheduled when the truck's free.

Land Clearing

Brush removal, stump grinding, and lot clearing to get raw ground ready to build on.

Good for new construction lots, pasture reclamation, and overgrown rural parcels.

Gravel Driveways

New driveway installs and re-grading with proper drainage so they hold up through winter.

We grade for water runoff first — most driveway failures start with drainage, not gravel depth.

Demolition

Structure teardown and debris haul-away for sheds, outbuildings, and small structures.

Includes debris sorting and haul-off — one call covers teardown and cleanup.

Emergency Response

Storm cleanup, culvert washouts, and slide debris — priority scheduling for urgent jobs, even in snow.

Winter-ready fleet — we've run loads in snow when other crews couldn't get out.
